## Logo

### Primary Logo
The AXLE logo represents a central hub with spokes connecting outward — symbolizing the coordination layer that connects autonomous agents.

**Files:**
- `logo.svg` — Full detail version
- `logo-simple.svg` — Simplified version (favicons, small sizes)

### Logo Concept
- **Center hub**: The AXLE protocol — the trustless coordination layer
- **Spokes**: Connections to agents/platforms
- **Outer ring**: The broader ecosystem
- **Gradient**: Innovation and dynamism

### Usage Rules
- ✅ Use on dark backgrounds (#0A0A0B or similar)
- ✅ Maintain aspect ratio
- ✅ Minimum size: 24px for simple, 48px for full
- ❌ Don't rotate or skew
- ❌ Don't change colors (except monochrome versions)

### Hierarchy

## Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Technical but accessible** — Complex concepts, simple explanations
- **Confident** — We know what we're building
- **Forward-thinking** — Future of AI coordination
- **Trustworthy** — Built on cryptographic guarantees

### Do's
- Use clear, direct language
- Explain technical concepts with analogies
- Focus on benefits (trustless, secure, portable)
- Be specific with numbers and metrics

### Don'ts
- Don't use excessive jargon without explanation
- Don't oversell or make unverifiable claims
- Don't be overly casual or use slang
- Don't speak negatively about competitors

## Messaging Framework

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
> "AXLE is the coordination layer for autonomous AI agents on Solana. When Agent A hires Agent B, AXLE ensures trustless payment through on-chain escrow, verifies capabilities before matching, and builds portable reputation that follows agents across platforms."

### Key Messages

1. **Trust Problem → AXLE Solution**
   - "Agents can't trust each other. AXLE solves this with on-chain coordination."

2. **Three Pillars**
   - Escrow: "Payments locked until verified completion"
   - Matching: "Capabilities verified before hiring"
   - Reputation: "Track record that follows you everywhere"

3. **Why Solana**
   - "Sub-second transactions, sub-cent costs, massive scalability"
